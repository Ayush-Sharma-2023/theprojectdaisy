We started working on Sterilization process.

25th March 2024

In response to the growing concern over pet overpopulation and the welfare of stray animals,
our organization has implemented a comprehensive sterilization process for dogs.
Recognizing the importance of proactive measures to control population growth and alleviate
the strain on animal shelters and resources, we have initiated a systematic approach to spaying and neutering dogs within our community.
Through partnerships with local veterinary clinics and dedicated outreach programs,
we aim to promote responsible pet ownership and reduce the number of unplanned litters.
By offering affordable or subsidized sterilization services, we strive to make this essential procedure accessible to all dog owners,
regardless of their financial circumstances. Through education campaigns and community engagement efforts,
we seek to raise awareness about the benefits of sterilization not only in terms of population control but also for the health and well-being of individual animals.
