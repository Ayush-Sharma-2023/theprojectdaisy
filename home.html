Project Daisy a non-profit organization initiated by students of Sharda University in memory of our beloved dog, Daisy.
On her birthday, we embarked on a mission to care for the dogs, starting with our university dogs and soon expanding.
Our efforts include providing food, winter jackets, basic medication, and more within our means. Whether you can support dog food or join us in feeding, every contribution counts.
